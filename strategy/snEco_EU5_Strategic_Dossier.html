Чотири стратегічні запитання

Q1 — Куди інвестувати найбільше у наступні 12 місяців?
🇩🇪 GERMANY — non-negotiable. Score 20/20: market size 5 + win probability 5 (NVC контракт підписано) + asset readiness 5 (SK plant + IFS Food v8 + FSSC) + strategic value 5 (DE wins unlock UK/FR/AT/CH per NVC exclusive).
→ Allocate 45% of total budget here.
Q2 — Найшвидша перемога (≤6 місяців до доходу)?
🇨🇿 CZECH REPUBLIC. Pitch Rohlik.cz (HQ Prague, 25k SKUs, 6–8 wk listing, no listing fee for niche brands). Geography 340 km from Humenné = 1-day truck. CZ TM №411165 ✅. Albert Excellent + M&S Food CZ warm targets.
→ Realistic first invoice: 60–90 days. Y1: €480–800k. Найвища ROAS з усіх 5 ринків.
Q3 — Найбільша довгострокова ставка (5-річна цінність)?
🇩🇪 DE (volume) + 🇫🇷 FR (brand).
DE = volume engine: €30–50M/yr by Y5 realistic on NVC + direct retail.
FR = brand engine: SIAL Paris home credential. Win in Carrefour Sélection + Monoprix Gourmet → unlock Harrods, Selfridges, Eataly, Whole Foods US.
→ 5-yr value if won: €15–20M/yr + 2× brand equity multiplier.
Q4 — Що деприоритизувати або відкласти?
🇳🇱 NETHERLANDS. Highest cheese consumption ≠ highest opportunity. AH 38% gatekeeper, 9–12 mo to listing, FrieslandCampina is BOTH supplier AND largest competitor (channel-conflict risk), Frico already owns "Dutch crunchy cheese".
→ Maintenance mode: Picnic + Crisp + Ekoplaza + cheese-specialty. Target €0.5–1M Y2. Defer AH/Jumbo until Y3 once DE/FR brand equity built. Save ~€200k.

⚠️ Critical regulatory finding
Slovak labels NOT acceptable in CZ. Czech SZPI requires Czech-language labels on consumer-final products. Action: produce dedicated CZ SKU. Translation cost minimal (~€500–1,000 one-time). Czech can sit alongside SK on multi-language pack.
